# Reclaiming Europe's Future

## Ave Europa

Europe’s political future is no longer decided in our parliaments alone. It is shaped in the algorithms of social media, where foreign powers, such as Russia, Iran, China, and the United States, deploy sophisticated networks of bots and paid influencers to manipulate public opinion. Their goal is clear: to divide us, distort our values, and erode trust in our institutions. While these actors wage digital warfare, Europe’s traditional political class remains slow, detached, and unprepared to counter the threat.

The result? Citizens are left exposed, their voices drowned out by foreign interference, and their ability to shape their own future compromised. Democracy itself is at stake.

At Ave Europa we are building the tools to change this.

We are developing the technology to detect and dismantle foreign disinformation campaigns, ensuring that European citizens, not foreign powers, control the narrative.
We are creating platforms that give citizens a direct voice, holding our political elites accountable.
And we are making sure Europe’s technological capabilities are not just reactive, but proactive, leading the charge in innovation, security, and digital resilience.

We are building the systems that ensure transparency, responsiveness, and accountability, so that decisions are made by Europeans, for Europeans.

The battle for Europe’s future is already underway.
We are here to make sure Europeans win.
Join our fight.
