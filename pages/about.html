About Me

Hey, I'm Andrea, a Computer Science student from Milan, Italy, with a passion for blending technology, politics, and cultural advocacy.

Growing up in a vibrant city like Milan, I've always been drawn to how digital tools can drive social change. As the Head of Tech at Ave Europa, a pan-European political movement, I lead a team in building scalable IT infrastructure, websites, and tools that promote a strong, democratic federal Europe. My work focuses on efficiency, security, and innovation to support grassroots activism across borders.

I'm also deeply involved with Associazione Anahita, an Iranian association in Italy dedicated to preserving Iran's rich cultural heritage while aiding the fight for freedom and democracy. This role has taught me the power of technology in amplifying voices and fostering community resilience.

Beyond my professional life, I enjoy exploring AI's ethical implications, contributing to open-source projects, and traveling to understand diverse perspectives. I'm committed to using tech for good, whether through coding political bots or analyzing NGO data.
